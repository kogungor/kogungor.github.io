About

With over 16 years of experience in cybersecurity, I began my professional career with cryptanalysis of image encryption in chaotic systems.

After working on offensive security and penetration testing at various companies, I served as an incident management specialist at multinational corporations. This gave me experience on both the offensive and defensive sides.

I've contributed as a technical reviewer to 5 internationally published books. I'm an interdisciplinary cybersecurity expert interested in the reflections of different disciplines in the cyber world, including terrorism and intelligence studies, psychological operations, and war studies.

In addition to technical certifications, I hold a Master's degree in International Relations and Terrorism, as well as various certifications related to terrorism and international security studies. On the physical security side, I have certifications in advanced tactical shooting, operational tactical shooting, dynamic carbine, tactical casualty care, and survival in extreme conditions.

I appeared as an assistant trainer and interrogation specialist in a reality show called Semender, broadcast on TRT Documentary channel. I've taught cryptology and system security courses at Marmara University Faculty of Engineering.

Currently, I'm conducting interdisciplinary cybersecurity research at Cyber Struggle and teaching an interdisciplinary and original course called Cyber War at Kadir Has University as part of the CyberMACs program, a joint master's program under the European Union.
